Facts About Cats

Cats are small, independent animals known for their playful and curious nature. They are popular pets because they are easy to care for and enjoy both attention and alone time. Cats often show affection by purring and staying close to their owners, making them loving companions.

Cats are gentle and curious animals that make wonderful pets. They love to explore their surroundings, play with small objects, and rest in warm, comfortable places. Even though they can be independent, cats often enjoy spending time with their owners and can be very affectionate. Their soft fur and calming purrs make them comforting companions in any home.
